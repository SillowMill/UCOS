title: "UCOS supports student occupations at universities"
category: "Statement"
date: "2024-06-05"
excerpt: "A clear position in support of academic freedom, peaceful protest, and global justice."
---

UCOS supports students engaging in peaceful collective action for justice and human rights.

We highlight the need for **critical dialogue**, protection of protest, and safe space for diverse voices in higher education.

Together with our partners, we continue to invest in education, decolonial practice, and structural change.
