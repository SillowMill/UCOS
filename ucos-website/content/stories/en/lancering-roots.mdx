title: "Launch of ROOTS: Students for Reproductive Justice"
category: "Program"
date: "2024-04-23"
excerpt: "ROOTS connects students around reproductive justice, inclusion, and collective impact."
---

With ROOTS, UCOS launched a student pathway focused on reproductive justice and global inequalities.

The program combines training, peer exchange, and local action with civil society partners.

The first cohort already designed concrete initiatives on access, stigma, and intersectional solidarity.
